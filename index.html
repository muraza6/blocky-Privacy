Blocky - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 24, 2025

Overview

Blocky values your privacy and is committed to protecting your data. This policy outlines what we collect, how it’s used, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Device Info: Model, OS version, and crash logs for technical support.
  • Gameplay Data: Level progress, scores, and achievements.

What We DO NOT Collect

  • No personal info (name, email, phone)
  • No camera, microphone, or contact access
  • No GPS or location tracking

How We Use Data

  • Improve game performance and balance
  • Debug crashes and enhance future updates
  • Save progress locally or via Google Play (if linked)

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Cloud save only if connected to Google Play
  • No third-party advertising or data sharing

Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Services for leaderboards and crash reporting
  • Google Analytics may be used (anonymously) to understand gameplay patterns

Children’s Privacy

Blocky is made for general audiences and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If any such data is discovered, it will be deleted promptly upon request.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy. All changes will be visible within the app or posted here with a revised date.
