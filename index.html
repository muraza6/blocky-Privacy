Block Puzzle - Classic Game - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 19, 2025

Overview

Block Puzzle - Classic Game values your privacy and is committed to protecting your data. This policy outlines what we collect, how it’s used, and your choices.

What We DO NOT Collect

  • We collect absolutely no data.
  • No personal info (name, email, phone)
  • No camera, microphone, or contact access
  • No GPS or location tracking

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Cloud save only if connected to Google Play

Third-Party Advertising (via AdMob Mediation)

We uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may, in turn, use mediation to serve ads from other networks, including Unity Ads and Meta Audience Network.

These third-party ad providers may collect limited, non-personally identifiable information automatically when ads are loaded, such as:

  • Device model and operating system
  • IP address (for coarse geographic region)
  • Advertising ID (e.g., Google Advertising ID)
  • App interaction data (e.g., ad clicks or impressions)

    • This data is used only by the ad networks for purposes such as:

  • Serving relevant advertisements
  • Measuring ad performance
  • Preventing fraud

Your Choices & Control

You can limit ad tracking on your Android device by:

  1. Going to Settings > Google > Ads
  2. Enabling “Opt out of Ads Personalization”
  3. Tapping “Reset advertising ID” to dissociate past activit

Children’s Privacy

Block Puzzle - Classic Game is made for general audiences and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If any such data is discovered, it will be deleted promptly upon request.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy. All changes will be visible within the app or posted here with a revised date.
